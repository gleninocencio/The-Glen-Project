MY STORY

Being in the corporate grind for the last decade, alienated me from my true self as an artist. I felt dejected and numb, and my creative self was stuck in a cage inside of me with the key thrown away.

I started to research figure drawing classes online, and I found an inexpensive, weekly session held on Saturday mornings in San Francisco. This is where it all began for me, and I rediscovered my creative voice again. Through self discovery, I started to draw what I felt, instead of what I see. I didn’t care as much about the technical aspects of drawing, instead I wanted to draw where my emotions and instincts took me for as long as the model held their pose.

I don’t know if I can make a living as an artist yet. I still have a foot in the corporate world and now I am tiptoeing in a new artistic venture. This is my experiment to see if I can fully put both feet in the art world and make things I love for a living. I want to share this journey.
